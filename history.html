He has lived a long time

Youth

Many years ago, before computers were commonplace (yes, there really was a time like that), the world was filled with uncertainty and woe. Famine ravaged the lands. Floods inundated cities. War scorched the earth, leaving nothing but death and disease in its wake.

Sages looked to the heavens for signs of change that they hoped would come. Scholars scoured their libraries and archives, looking in vain for a way to eradicate the horrors which plagued all of mankind. World leaders wrung their hands as they were powerless in the face of such devastating misery. Yes, the times were very bad, and a lot of people were really mad.
